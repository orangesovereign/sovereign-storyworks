Sovereign Storyworks - voice files (E2)

Drop creator-recorded .ogg files here and reference them by filename in a
talk task's dialogue lines, e.g. "voice": "ezra_greeting_01.ogg".
Files play through the HUD NUI at the volume set in config_runtime.lua
(ConfigRuntime.Dialogue.voiceVolume). Keep filenames to letters, numbers,
dots, dashes and underscores.
